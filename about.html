ABOUT ME

Pelin Sen, born on July 1, 2001, graduated in 2025 from the Visual Communication Design department at Kadir Has University. After completing her education, she opened her own gallery, aiming to inspire art enthusiasts. With a passion for visual arts and a unique perspective, Pelin strives to bring art to a wider audience and provides a platform for emerging talents in her gallery.
